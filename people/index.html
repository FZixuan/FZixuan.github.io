Welcome to my homepage! In Fall 2023, I started my PhD program in the Department of Industrial and Systems Engineering at the University of Florida. I’m working with Dr. Aleksandr Kazachkov in the Center of Applied Optimization. My research interests include helping MIP (Mixed Integer Programming) solver making smarter decisions and computational economics (fair devision, etc.)

I spend my spare time on dancing (chinese dance, ballet basics, sportive latin american ballroom dance), listening to music, making handicrafts, and playing with my cat Yuanxiao .

I’m currently the secretary of the INFORMS Student Chapter at UF. Feel free to reach out to me if you are interested in my research, my hobbies, our student chapter, or just want to say hi!
